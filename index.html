About Me

Hello! My name is Lukasz Dworakowski (as you can probably tell from me pasting it all over the place), and I am currently a student at the University of Toronto Mississauga studying Computer Science, Mathematics, and Statistics. Team Member

I worked as a team member in a Tim hortons, this included taking orders, making orders, dealing with customers, etc.

University of Toronto - Autonomous Systems and Biomechatronics Laboratory

Summer student: Reasearch Assistant

I had a summer placement at the U of T ASB research lab, this placement included: modifying the AirSim plugin developed by Microsoft, albeit a much earlier version than is available now, to be capable of simulating autonomous ground vehicles. I maintained, and modified, the various C++ components of my fork of the plugin. I was also responsible for adapting a plugin for the Gazebo simulation software that would generate random training environments of varying difficulties in order to assist in training an ground vehicle AI. I created python scripts in order to automate the task, as well as created very basic "base models" for terrain in the maps.
